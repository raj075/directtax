Payroll

  • We provide a complete payroll service running from weekly, monthly, quarterly or another payroll. All you need to do is tell us about your staffs salary, then we calculate PAYE tax, NI contribution and finally net pay to your staff. We prepare payslips and other reports using HMRC approved software. We also provide expert advice on the amount payable to HMRC and the deadline for that payment.
  • The employer will need to file a P35 return which shows the payroll details for the year ending 5th April, and to HMRC by the 19th July.
