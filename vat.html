VAT

  • We help prepare your VAT return and submit to HMRC. Based on your business situation, the VAT returns will need to be filed monthly or quarterly to HMRC. As this is a regulatory requirement, it is essential this is done to ensure your business is not penalised or in worst case, closed.
  • HMRC provides various VAT schemes, and with our experience, we will help you decide which scheme is right for your business. Choosing the appropriate VAT scheme, your business can save you money.
  • It is vital to claim the right amount of input tax you have spent in your purchases and expenses, and to declare the correct output tax on your sales. If the input tax is higher than your output tax, HMRC will refund you the difference which can help your business cash flow.
