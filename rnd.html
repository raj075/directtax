Research And Development

  • As a company, you can claim for R&D Relief if an R&D project is to achieve an advancement in knowledge or capability in a field of either science or technology. R&D Relief can be generous as you can claim 200% relief for a qualified expenditure, however there are certain conditions which must be fulfilled:
  • It must be directly related to your company’s trade – must be either an existing one or one intended to start up based on results of the R&D
  • If your company is claiming relief under the SME Scheme, your company must own any logical property that might arise from the project
  • The procedures can be very complicated, however, with our expertise, we will fulfil those complications which will allow you to successfully claim your R&D relief
